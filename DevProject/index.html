Welcome to PoweResume

PoweResume aims to restore the balance of power back to candidates in the interview process. This is achieved by:

  • Using the algorithyms that companies use to screen resumes against them to ensure that your resume is at, or near, the top of the pile reviewed
  • Comparing your resume to the job description for the role that you are applying for and offering guidance on how to improve the symmetry between the two
  • Offering guidance on salary expectations based on those who have held similar positions in your area
